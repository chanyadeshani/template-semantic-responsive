Action for children

This charity take action so children don't miss out on a safe and happy childhood. They have thousands of people giving children and young people the protection and practical help they need. Action for children have been helping children for more than 150 years. Today, they have 372 services across the UK, in local communities, in schools and onliner

Last year, they helped over 54,878 young people with their mental health and wellbeing. They reached 8,000 young people through The Blue's Programme. 75% said they had increased confidence, 73% increased seld esteem, and 81% were now able to talk about their mental health and wellbeing. 98% would recommend the service to a friend

Action for children work in communities to offer practical and caring support to children and families. They offer a huge range of support including help for families with disabled children, and servises for young carers. It includes free or low-cost parenting classes through their children's centres
